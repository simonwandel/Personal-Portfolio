Java Software Engineer in Financial Services

Hello, I am Simon - a young software engineer from Tübingen, Germany. Currently, I am located in Düsseldorf working for a consulting company.

At the age of 12 I started to get in touch with code. However, my ant simulation software I was trying to build with the software language C# wasn't working properly. I got so frustrated that I decided that software development is definitely not something I am suitable for. Well, I was wrong. A couple of years later I finally fell in love with coding. I started developing Windows Phone Apps (yes, that's history now - I know). Eventually, my most popular app - a cognitive skills enhancement app - got so popular that I founded a company for it.

In the last couple of years I was able to work with the coolest companiest building the coolest products. I was not only able to build more apps, but also got in touch with augmented reality, artificial intelligence, cloud, microservices and so much more.

Currently, I am working for a consulting company gaining more experience working with financial companies on their software.
